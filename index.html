About Me

I'm a lifelong Memphian with a wife and two sons under 10. I have work with the earth all my adult life as an archeologist, earth scientist, and geospatial developer. Besides map development, I'm also into video and sound editing as well as reactive web design. Right now, I am obsessed with the advancement in AI: its possibilities for the GIS field and its impact on the future.
